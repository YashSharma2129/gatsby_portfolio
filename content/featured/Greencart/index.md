**Problem:** Logistics companies need intelligent driver assignment and live delivery tracking to optimize routes and reduce operational costs.

**Architecture:** Full-stack logistics platform with intelligent driver assignment algorithm, live GPS tracking, and **role-based dashboards** for admins, drivers, and customers. Built with TypeScript end-to-end.

**Key Decision:** Used MongoDB with geospatial indexing for location-based driver matching. Implemented real-time tracking using WebSocket connections for live delivery updates.

**Impact:** Improved delivery efficiency by **25%** through automated driver-delivery matching.
